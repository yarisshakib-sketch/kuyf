# KUYF Football Tournament

Official landing page for the **Kaji Bari United Youth Foundation (KUYF) Football Tournament** — built to get players excited and signed up.

🔗 **Live site:** [https://yarisshakib-sketch.github.io/football.kuyf/](https://yarisshakib-sketch.github.io/football.kuyf/)

## About the Tournament

- **Format:** Round-robin league — every team plays every team, top 2 teams meet in the turf final
- **Squad size:** 6 starters + 1 extra man (7 players per team)
- **Positions:** Category A, B, C, D, E, and G (Goalkeeper)
- **Expected players:** ~30
- **Registration fee:** ৳800/team (if 5 teams enroll) or ৳1000/team (if 4 teams enroll)
- **Ground:** Primarily near the Bay of Bengal Dam, final on turf (budget permitting)

## Tech Used

- Single-file **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript** — no frameworks or build steps
- **Google Fonts** (Orbitron + Rajdhani) for the scoreboard/athletic look
- Neon floodlight-style visuals, hover/glow interactions, and scroll-reveal animations
- Hosted for free on **GitHub Pages**

## Updating the Site

- Edit `index.html` directly on GitHub (or upload a new version) — Pages redeploys automatically within a minute or two
- To connect the registration form, find the `TODO: replace "#" with your Google Form link` comment in `index.html` and update the `href`
